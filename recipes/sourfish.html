What is suan cai yu?

Szechuan style fish stew with pickled mustard greens. This is not a famous dish but I feel something like it is my responsibility to introduce this dish to Szechuan style food lovers.

Ingredients

  • Raw fish
  • sour vegetable
  • Other favouring you want to add
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Add the oil.
  2. Puting meat and vegetabble inside.
  3. Turn on the heating cook for at least 30 mins.
  4. Enjoy your food.
